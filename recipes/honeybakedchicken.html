Honey Baked Chicken

Description

Great Recipe for a honey baked chicken.

Contains high amounts of protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup honey
  • ¼ cup prepared mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken pieces in a shallow baking pan, skin side up.
  3. Combine the melted butter or margarine, honey, mustard, salt and curry powder
  4. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 1/4 hours (75 minutes), basting every 15 minutes with pan drippings, until the chicken is nicely browned and tender and the juices run clear.
